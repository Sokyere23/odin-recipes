Ghanian Fried Rice

Home

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Garlic, Ginger, Onion, Scallion
  • Cabbage and Carrot
  • Soy sauce and Sesame Oil
  • Chicken Seasoning
  • White Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Scramble the eggs and set aside
  3. Stir fry the garlic, ginger, onion, green onion, cabbage and carrot.
  4. Add the soy sauce, pepper and chicken seasoning
  5. Stir fry the garlic and ginger. Add in the rice. Stir.
  6. Add in the soy sauce and sesame oil. Stir.
  7. Add the veggies. Stir.
  8. Turn the stove off, add in the eggs and green onions. Stir.
