Macaroni and Cheese

Home

Mac and cheese is a dish made with macaroni pasta covered in a cheese sauce, typically using cheddar cheese, and usually baked in the oven; it's considered a classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter and flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil and drain macaroni per box instructions.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet.
  3. Sprinkle in flour and stir until pasty.
  4. Whisk in warmed milk and let cook until thickened.
  5. Add cheese to sauce.
  6. Add sauce to pasta.
